NAME

       showvec - turn display of particle vectors off or on

SYNOPSIS

       showvec yes/no

ALIASES

       svec

DESCRIPTION

       showvec  takes  a yes or no argument.  The yes argument allows vectors
       of particles to be plotted.  The vector quantities  of  the  particles
       are  input  using  the readvector command.  showvel and showvec cannot
       both be yes.  The length of  the  vectors  are  controlled  using  the
       velscale  command.   The no argument causes vectors not to be plotted.
       This is the default.  The arguments yes or no can be abbreviated y  or
       n.

SEE ALSO

       showstar(1), showvel(1), velscale(1)
