NAME

       boxstat - print the physical parameters of a box

SYNOPSIS

       boxstat box-number particle

DESCRIPTION

       boxstat  prints  physical parameters of the specified box, box-number.
       These parameters are for particles within the box  of  type  particle.
       Parameters  that  are listed are: the mass, the density, the volume of
       the box, the size of the box, the center of mass coordinates, the cen-
       ter  of  mass  velocity, and the angular momentum per unit mass in the
       center of mass frame.  If the box is not loaded an error is generated.

OPTIONS

   Particle
       The possible choices for particle are:

              all
                 Show  the  physical  parameters  of all the particles in box
                 box-number

              star
                 Show the physical parameters of only the star and gas parti-
                 cles in box box-number

              dark
                 Show  the physical parameters of only the dark matter parti-
                 cles in box box-number

              gas
                 Show the physical parameters of only the  gas  particles  in
                 box box-number

              star
                 Show  the  physical parameters of only the star particles in
                 box box-number
