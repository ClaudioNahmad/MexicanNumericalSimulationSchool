NAME

       catbox - concatenate two boxes into a third

SYNOPSIS

       catbox box1 box2 box3

DESCRIPTION

       catbox  is  used  to  concatenate  the particles in box1 and box2 into
       box3.  Particles that are duplicated in box1 and box2 will be  noticed
       and only copied once.

       All  the boxes must be between 1 and MAXBOX or an error will be gener-
       ated.  The value of MAXBOX can be found by using the version  command.
