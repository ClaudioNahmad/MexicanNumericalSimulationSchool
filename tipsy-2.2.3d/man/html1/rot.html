NAME

       rotate - rotate the viewing angle

SYNOPSIS

       rotate direction degrees

DESCRIPTION

       rotate is a command to rotate the viewing angle degrees degress in the
       direction direction.  degrees may be either positive or negative.   To
       view  the  particles from the new direction the rotate command must be
       followed by either the viewall, viewarray, viewgas, viewmag,  viewpot,
       viewrad,  or viewstar commands.  The clearrot command resets the view-
       ing angle to be looking down the z-axis.  The angleup command can also
       be  used  to  rotate  the simulation depending on the system's angular
       momentum.

OPTIONS

   Direction
       The possible choices for direction are:

              left
                 Rotate the viewing angle to the left degrees degrees.   This
                 option may be abbreviated l

              right
                 Rotate the viewing angle to the right degrees degrees.  This
                 option may be abbreviated r

              up Rotate the viewing angle up degrees  degrees.   This  option
                 may be abbreviated u

              down
                 Rotate  the viewing angle down degrees degrees.  This option
                 may be abbreviated d

              clockwise
                 Rotate the viewing angle clockwise  degrees  degrees.   This
                 option may be abbreviated c
