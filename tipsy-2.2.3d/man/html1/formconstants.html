NAME

       formconstants - set the constants related to star formation

SYNOPSIS

       formconstants cstar dtime temppar softpar

ALIASES

       fconst

DESCRIPTION

       formconstants  sets  the value of constants that relate to star forma-
       tion for use in commands such as viewgas.  The  star  formation  algo-
       rithm  is  from Katz (Ap. J. 391:502, 1992).  All the constants are in
       system units with G (the gravitational constant) equal to 1  and  tem-
       perature in degrees Kelvin.

              cstar
                 is  the  constant  that is linearly proportional to the star
                 formation rate.

              dtime
                 is the time between calls to the star formation routine.

              temppar
                 is the constant that decides how cool the gas can be  before
                 the cooling time is ignored.

              softpar
                 is the constant that decides when the Jean's criteria can be
                 ignored.
