NAME

       coolconstants - set the values of constants related to cooling

SYNOPSIS

       coolconstants kpcunit msolunit fhydrogen

ALIASES

       cconst

DESCRIPTION

       coolconstants  sets  the value of constants that relate to cooling for
       use in commands such as viewgas.  All  the  constants  are  in  system
       units  with  G (the gravitational constant) equal to 1 and temperature
       in degrees Kelvin.

              kpcunit
                 is the system length unit in kiloparsecs.

              msolunit
                 is the system mass unit in solar masses.

              fhydrogen
                 is the fraction of the gas by weight in hydrogen in the sys-
                 tem.  the density.
