NAME

       unzoom - reset zoom factor to 1

SYNOPSIS

       unzoom

DESCRIPTION

       unzoom  resets  the  zoom factor of the display to 1.  This undoes the
       effect of the zoom command and the zoom scrollbar.

SEE ALSO

       zoom(1)
