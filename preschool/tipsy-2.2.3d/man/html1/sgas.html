NAME

       showgas - turn display of gas particles off or on

SYNOPSIS

       showgas yes/no

ALIASES

       sgas

DESCRIPTION

       showgas  takes a yes or no argument.  The yes argument allows gas par-
       ticles to be plotted.  This is the default.  The  no  argument  causes
       gas  particles  not  to  be  plotted.   The arguments yes or no can be
       abbreviated y or n.
