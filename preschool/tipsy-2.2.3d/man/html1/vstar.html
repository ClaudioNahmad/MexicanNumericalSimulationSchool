NAME

       viewstar - plot particles according to star properties

SYNOPSIS

       viewstar plot_type min max [clip]

ALIASES

       vstar

DESCRIPTION

       viewstar  plots the star particles colored according to the star prop-
       erty specified by plot_type.  Plot_type is one of  luminosityv,  lumv,
       (V  band  luminosity) luminosityb, lumb, (B band luminosity) luminosi-
       tyu, lumu, (U band luminosity) luminosityuu, lumuu, (UU band  luminos-
       ity)  magnitudev,  magv,  (V band magnitude) magnitudeb, magb, (B band
       magnitude) magnitudeu, magu, (U band  magnitude)  magnitudeuu,  maguu,
       (UU  band  magnitude) tform, (formation time) logtform, (log(formation
       time)) age, logage.  logmass, rho, (density) or logrho (log(density)).
       min  and  max  determine  the  value of the minimum and maximum color,
       respectively.


       clip can be either cliplo, clipup, or clipboth  to  suppress  plotting
       particles either below the minimum, above the maximum, or both.
